# Implementation Tips

- Consistency is key!
- 4× or 3× per day is probably fine
- Aim for ~800-900 identifications / week
    - 40-45 × 3
    - 30-35 × 4
    - 25 × 5
- Tie practice times to other events (e.g. "before school", "before dinner", etc)
- Try to get the kids excited about it
